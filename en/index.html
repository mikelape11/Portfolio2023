About me.

My name is Mikel Apezetxea, I was born in 1999 and I am from Euskadi/Basque Country (Spain). I am a designer focused on UI/UX design, visual design, web design and prototyping. My goals are to help clients make a difference in the market with my design skills, doing clean and organized work and keep learning daily.

I studied web and cross-platform application development for 4 years. As I progressed with my studies, I immediately realized that what I really liked was everything related to the world of design.

What I do.

Web/Application design

I create all kinds of websites or custom prototypes for all applications.

Responsive design

Todos los diseños son adaptables a diferentes dispositivos, moviles, tabletas o portatiles.

SEO - Search Engine Optimization

I optimize websites so that they appear as high as possible in Internet search browsers.
